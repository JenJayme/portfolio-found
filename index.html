    Bio

    I'm enjoying a second career in full-stack web development after some years in fundraising, marketing and communications for great causes.

    I've served in leadership roles at several prominent organizations including Big Brothers Big Sisters, Community Action Marin, Hispanic Scholarship Fund, St. Vincent de Paul Society, and Santa Clara University, and consulted with many others. I've managed numerous major projects from start to finish, launched new websites and brand strategies, implemented various new technologies, started new programs, led teams to meet ambitious fundraising goals, and inspired millions of dollars in donations.

    Along the way, I got to learn from some amazing people who practiced transformative methods including Growth Mindset, Lean Management, Kaizen, User-Centered Design and Agile Development. These principles have cultivated my exceptional leadership, project management, communication and interpersonal skills.

    I've frequently served as the resident website administrator, database builder, early adopter of new technologies and all-around tech whiz in thinly staffed nonprofits. In 2020, when the pandemic shook up life as we know it, I embraced the moment to deepen my technical skills at UC Berkeley’s Coding Boot Camp.

    I’m most proficient in HTML, CSS, Javascript and React, but continuously learning and comfortable with many technologies including server-side and database architectures such as MySql, MongoDB and Node/Express servers.

    I’m actively exploring chances to exercise and deepen my coding skills, whether in consulting gigs, pro bono work, or employment opportunities.
